In the winter of 2020, as group gatherings had shut down, Alaska Youth Soccer hosted a successful FIFA 20 Esports tournament. Members around the state registered and participated in tournaments on both XBOX One and PlayStation 4, and first place champions received prizes from HyperX and Chipotle.

 

We are continuing to evaluate holding similar events in the future. Announcements will be made and this page will be updated as we develop more concrete plans, so stay tuned!
