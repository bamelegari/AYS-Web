Getting Started

The easiest way to get started is to head to https://www.aksra.org/becoming-a-referee. This page will walk you through the steps necessary to become a referee as well as present you with a form to get you on the mailing list designed specifically for people who are interested in becoming a referee.

If you have any questions, or want to know about where to get games, it is best to contact your District Referee Administrator. Contact info for all Alaska referee administrators can be found at the Alaska Soccer Referee Association "Contact Us" page. If an administrator is currently not listed for your region, contact the State Youth Referee Administrator.

The Alaska Soccer Referee Association website, aksra.org, has many other resources for referees as well.
