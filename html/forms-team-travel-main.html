• Annual Team Application to Travel - Teams traveling will apply for the Annual Travel Permit for all US Youth Soccer events/tournaments. Valid Sept 1-Aug 31, approval for one year. Not required for Far West Regionals or President's Cup teams. Payment must be received at time of submission. Pay here.
  • Auto Insurance Request - Teams wishing to purchase coverage for vehicles rented for travel. Please read the instructions carefully.
  • Application for Travel to Foreign Country - USSF-required. 60 Days Prior to teams traveling to a foreign country, including Mexico and Canada. Must be submitted with Annual Team Application to Travel and Ted Stevens Amateur Act Form.
  • Ted Stevens Amateur Act - Submit with "Application For Travel To Foreign Country" - teams/players traveling overseas to play soccer.
