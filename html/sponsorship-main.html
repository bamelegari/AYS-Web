The Alaska Youth Soccer Association (AYSA), a 501(C)(3) nonprofit, is a member of the United States Soccer Federation and US Youth Soccer, and is a mission-based association providing leadership, administrative state level programs, and education and development to our members and the public about the game of soccer within coordination of youth soccer in the State of Alaska.

A message to partners from the Board of Directors

Sponsoring AYSA

AYSA is pleased to offer several advertising opportunities to meet your marketing objectives and, more importantly, your budget! Our advertising options are reaching more people than ever across multiple channels and not only is your contribution tax deductible, your business exposure will also reach thousands across the state of Alaska. Sponsors enjoy maximum exposure through social media, our statewide website, banners, field signs, tournaments, and social events.

Leader in Growth

AYSA serves 16 affiliate member associations and clubs in Alaska with over 9,000 registered players, coaches, officials, volunteers, and league administrators in the Interior, Anchorage, Mat-Su, Kenai Peninsula, and the Southeast. Youth soccer is growing in our state with the implementation of new statewide programs; why not be a part of the excitement!

Messaging Opportunities
  • Website Advertising on www.alaskayouthsoccer.org: Market your company and/or product to the thousands of players, coaches and parents who visit the official AYSA website for ongoing information and updates regularly.
  • Tournament and Events Advertising: Market your company and/or product to players, coaches, and parents who participate in Statewide Tournaments and Events, including:
    • The Alaska Development Program: The Alaska Development Program (ADP) and US Youth Soccer Olympic Development Program (ODP) is an Alaska Youth Soccer run umbrella program offering statewide high-level training opportunities for Alaska players.
    • Alaska State Cup Tournament (packages): The US Youth Soccer Alaska State Cup Tournament showcases the highest level of competitive play within the state. The tournament is structured to determine which teams advance to the US Youth Soccer Championships.
    • Alaska Futsal League: The Alaska Futsal League (AFL) is an Alaska Youth Soccer-run umbrella program offering statewide futsal instruction, player opportunities, league games, tournaments, coach development, and referee development. The statewide futsal league serves as a venue and a pathway for the state's youth soccer players to further develop their skills at all levels while providing opportunities to become elite players at the state level along with the promotion of club development and growth.
    • Many more (general event packages)
