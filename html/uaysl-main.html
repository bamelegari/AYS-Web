The United Anchorage Youth Soccer League (UAYSL) is the largest competitive youth soccer league in Anchorage, Alaska. UAYSL is comprised of member youth competitive clubs located in Anchorage, Eagle River/Chugiak, and the Matanuska-Susitna Valley. UAYSL offers three competitive leagues per soccer year:

  • Indoor: Early Junuary - Early April
  • Summer Outdoor: Late May - July
  • Fall Outdoor: Late August - Early October

UAYSL is a AYSA-sanctioned league and Associate Member of AYSA.

For more information and registration, visit the UAYSL Website
