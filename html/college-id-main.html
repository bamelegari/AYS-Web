Alaska Youth Soccer College ID Camp

College Coach Recruiting Forum

The Alaska Youth Soccer College ID Camp brings high-level training sessions to over 100 high school players from colleges looking to recruit Alaskan soccer players at schools that best fit Alaskans and where Alaskans have had successful players and academic careers. More information will be posted regarding the 2023 College ID camp when it becomes available.

If you have questions, contact Camp Director Dan Rufner at dan@adpsoccer.org or 907-764-2567.
