2023 Alaska Youth Soccer College ID Camp

College Coaches Recruiting Forum June 7

Morning and Afternoon Training Sessions June 8-10

Camp Fee: $300

West Anchorage High School and Romig Middle School turf fields

REGISTER

The Alaska Youth Soccer College ID Camp brings high-level training sessions to over 100 high school players from colleges looking to recruit Alaskan soccer players at schools that best fit Alaskans and where Alaskans have had successful players and academic careers.

Registration is open to all incoming freshmen through recent graduates. No club affiliation is required. The camp fee of $300 includes the coach recruiting forum, six training sessions, and a camp training tee.
