When submission of your completed AYSA Form (with appropriate signatures if required), and payment (by check or online) have been successfully received, the AYSA Office will process and provide notification within three to five business days. AYSA forms are to be submitted to alaskayouthsoccer@gmail.com for processing.

Payment by check only is required for the following forms:

  • Outdoor Tournament Hosting Application
  • Sanctioned Event Hosting Application
  • Annual Application to Facilitate a Sanctioned League

Payment by card or check is required for the following forms:

  • Player Release and Transfer Form
  • Team Annual Travel Permit
  • State Cup Release and Transfer
  • President's Cup Release and Transfer
  • Annual Non-member Membership
