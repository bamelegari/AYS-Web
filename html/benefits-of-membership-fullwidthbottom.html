Automatic Member Benefits

Included with yearly registration

  • Registration services
  • Insurance (secondary medical and liability)
  • US Youth Soccer and USSF membership
  • Free coaching education
  • Risk management
  • Processing adult volunteer background check
  • Sanctioning of guest play, travel, and tournaments
  • USSF referee education
  • Publications
  • Weekday business hours

  • The Alaska Development Program (ADP)/Olympic Development Program (ODP) for elite players
  • Participation in the Far West Regional League (FWRL)
  • TOPSoccer for those with disabilities
  • Alaska State Cup and the National Championship Series
  • Alaska Soccer Referee Administration education and certification
  • Alaska Futsal League (AFL)
  • College tour for high school players/sub-regional residential camp
  • Yearly recognition awards for coaches, administrators, volunteers, and referees
  • Yearly educational clinics and trainings
  • Player development opportunities
  • Foraker Group: club affiliate members (reduced rates for services)
Statewide Player Opportunities

US Youth Soccer and Alaska Youth Soccer enable players and teams to participate in local leagues, regional leagues, and the premier National League. College coaches are present at the Region ODP tournaments, the Western Region Championships, and the National Championships.

Player and Coach Risk Management

US Youth Soccer and AYSA provides a complete service of Risk Management for your club, providing training and safety materials on goal security, sexual abuse awareness, concussion protocol/awareness, and rest and recovery for player development.

Coach Education

Members have access to statewide coaching seminars. The Alaska State Director of Coaching will be available to help develop club coaches/volunteers. Annual Youth module training provided by AYSA for recreational coaches at no cost to the coach or club. Ability to register/attend USSF license courses, grassroots coaching courses and the AYSA Coaching Enhancement Grant Program.

Administrative Services

Members have access to the AYSA State Office for support and guidance throughout the year. The dedicated staff will provide real time assistance to clubs and administrators. The AYSA office can assist clubs with writing and revising bylaws, board support, facilitating claims for injuries, providing guidance, and overall club support, along with processing AYSA club/player/team forms, and yearly adult volunteer backgrounds checks. Your programs, players, administrators, facilities, camps, and tournaments will be insured through your Alaska Youth Soccer membership fees.
