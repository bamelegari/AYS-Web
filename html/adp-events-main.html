Team Selection Camp

June 5-7, 2023

Anchorage, AK

West Anchorage High School and Romig Middle School

The Team Selection Camp is for all players that have been scouted or recommended and invited to attend in the 2012-2009 age groups. This camp is invitation only and will serve as the opportunity for the coaching staff to select the teams for the upcoming events. We will select 32 players (2 teams, 24 total for the 2012 group) for the Training Camp and the Starfire Labor Day Cup and then from there we will select the 1 team of 16 that will attend the ODP Championships in Phoenix.
ADP Futures Camp

June 5-7, 2023

Anchorage, AK

West Anchorage High School and Romig Middle School

This year we will also be hosting an ADP Futures Camp for the 2013 and 2014 age groups. This camp will run alongside the Team Selection Camp for the 2012-2009 players. This camp is for the players to experience the ADP program and staff, they will not be chosen for teams or sent to events after this camp.
