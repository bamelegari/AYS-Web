Alaska Youth Soccer statewide affiliate clubs and leagues will follow ALL federal and municipality mandates/emergency orders, and Division of Public Health guidelines for the appropriate number of people in one group, distancing 6-10 feet apart, and face covering guidelines.

Priority for adherence to guidelines from different levels will follow as: 1) Municipality EO 2) Facility Mitigation Guidelines 3) AYSA Indoor Mitigation Guidelines.

Alaska Youth Soccer remains steadfast in our commitment to the safety of our players, coaches, referees, families, and communities. We appreciate your help in preventing the spread of COVID-19 and appreciate your patience in this matter!
