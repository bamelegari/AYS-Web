Alaska Airlines Cup

TBD, 2023.

Alaska Rush: "Teams from Canada and the U.S. participate each year making this the largest tournament in Alaska. The Alaska Airlines Cup features six collegiate or professional level referees flown in by our generous sponsor, Alaska Airlines. All teams are assured four games. Based in Anchorage, the majority of games will be played at Kincaid Park and Stadium. The setting is second to none with gorgeous views of alpine peaks surrounded by the water of Cook Inlet."


Zane Memorial Cup

June 29 - July 2, 2023. Russel Oberg Soccer Park, Chugiak.

Chugiak Soccer Club: "The SSAAK Zane Cup tournament started in 2006 as an academy and recreational team event. It transitioned to Select Competitive only in 2009 and expanded the age groups to include U13 and U17 Competitive. Held in July of each year, the Zane Cup has grown to one of the largest Summer end of season tournaments in Alaska with over 60 teams from Alaska. This is a great post-season tournament to wrap up the soccer year or prepare for the Alaska Youth Soccer Association State Cup."
